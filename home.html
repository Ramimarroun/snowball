Work for from your home

Our website offers people in poor and developing countries opportunities to get a job while they are working at home

We believe in your skills

Many of you have different experience and skills, but you live in a country which is affected of poverty. but we believe that you do have a lot of skills that can make en income internaly in your country and abroad. We have contact with many companies and factories around the world which are ready to offer people work based on their requirements. The main plan of our plan IS to distribute jobs to make them available to everybody in the world who lives in one of the developing countries, and would you like to work at home, you can join us. Please check you if your countries in the developing list

Who can apply for a job

We don’t claim from you any recognized certification or Education. but we need to know more about your skills and possibilities. for example, if you like to take photos, we can help you to distribute your work and get them sold in the market. if you are a designer and want to make clothing, we can help you with types of equipment, like drawing and sewing. if you are a handyman and can produce handicrafts like pottery and so on, we can help with the equipment you need and how to sell them in the market. we are in contact with the government, banks, ideal organization and many of these offering microloans. that means you get a loan to start your business and are paying back when your business is doing well and you earn money.

Identity

You have to prove your identity, your address, telephone number, and your bank account. We are aware that some people don’t have a bank account, so we can send you the money by any available transfer money serves such as Western Union or M-Pesa Mobile Wallets.

What kind of qualifications do we need?

We accept all from you. Whatever you are, teacher, designer, photographer, translator, programmer, and other careers even Handicrafts. You can check the list of you can find your interests. Note that may you don’t find your job, so you can contact us by email and describe what you can do, so we can add your job to the list.
